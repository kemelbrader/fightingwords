In the summer of 2012 the exhibition Fighting Words: American Revolutionary War Pamphlets featured 41 pamphlets from the University of Utah's rare book collections. The exhibition chronicled the war from both sides of the Atlantic, using direct quotes from first editions by Benjamin Franklin, Thomas Paine, William Pitt, and others.

This website transforms the physical exhibition into a collaborative, multimedia, online exhibition.

The 41 videos are the result of a collaboration between Alison Conner, University of Utah Rare Books Curator; Sarah Shippobotham, Associate Professor and Head of the Actor Training Program; 11 students from the Theater department; and 2 students from the Film and Media Arts department at the University of Utah.
